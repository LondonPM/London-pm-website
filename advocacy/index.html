Companies who use Perl within London.

Intro to this section

London is home to many companies which are using Perl - both in existing and new projects.

The London Perl Mongers have put together this list of just some of these companies as a demonstration of how widespread and varied the usage of Perl is.

If you represent a company that uses Perl and would like to be included on this page, please contact the webmaster including a logo, url and short description of your company.
