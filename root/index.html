Who are we?

We are a group of people dedicated to the encouragement of all things to do with the Perl programming language in London, UK. This involves helping each other, discussing topics, sharing information and the occasional meeting.

We are part of the world-wide Perl Mongers group and play host to (as well as visit) others from round the world.

Where are we?

They seek us here, they seek us there...

There is an active mailing list and an IRC channel. Check these for up-to-date information on physical or virtual meetings.

There are technical talk videos available on the London PM TV Channel
